Thank you from the bottom of our s!

What you helped achieve for Ixpaco:
  • About 200 pounds of toothpaste, toothbrushes and dental floss were donated. This amounts to 4 full-size (50 lbs) suitcases.
  • Over 100 new books in Spanish were purchased for the local elementary school. For many of the students, these will be the first story and non fiction books that they will ever hold in their hands.
  • Every student, from the local elementary school, will once again own a text book! The school year started in mid January but not all students attend the first few weeks of school. Therefore, the textbooks will be purchased in February. $200 were reserved for these; the amount that was needed last year to ensure all students had a textbook.
  • Monetary donations also helped us ship/fly the dental supplies and books to Guatemala. Can you picture my family of 4 with 12 suitcases? It was totally worth the effort!

What's next?

The tin roof needs to be replaced. It leaks and students spend more than an hour, during the winter mornings, drying out their classrooms.

There isn't waste management in Ixpaco and no coffee plantation owner wants these old desks on their lands. They are taking room in the classrooms and are dangerously piled hi. They need to be returned to the school district and a big truck needs to be rented to do so.
